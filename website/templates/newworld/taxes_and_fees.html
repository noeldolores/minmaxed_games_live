Taxes & Fees


The ability to view your Territory Standing bonuses seems to still be bugged or removed, so here are some workarounds:
Refining Station: Please visit a workstation to view your taxes for refining. Mouse over each tier to see an item list.
Trading Tax: Open the world map and select your city. View your tax rate on the right-side panel next to the coin icon.
Standing Discount: Visit the Trading Post and select an item that is at least 100g. To calculate your discount:
Territory Standing Discount / Listing Fee. Example: 2148.91 / 11250 = 19.1
Listing Duration: This sets the default for all calculators / tools. The shorter the duration, the cheaper your fees will be.
Company Owned Territory: Grants a 30% discount to trading post and workstation fees within your territory.
First Light Control: Grants a +10% output bonus to crafting / refining.
The data you enter will be saved to your browser for future visits.
